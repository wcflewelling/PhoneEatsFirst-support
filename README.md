## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I add friends?
Tap the **Friends** tab at the bottom, then search for someone by username. Tap **Add** to send a friend request.

### How does the free tier work?
The free version lets you post **3 meals per day** and view **today only**. Upgrade to **Me Gusta Pro** (one-time $2.99 purchase) for unlimited posts and full meal history.

### How do I delete my account?
Go to **Profile → ⋯ menu → Settings & Privacy → Delete Account**. This permanently removes your account, all posts, and friend connections.

### How do I block or report someone?
Tap the **⋯** on any post to **Report Post** or **Block User**. Blocked users disappear from your feed immediately.

### My streak disappeared!
Streaks reset if you miss a day. Open the app and post at least one meal every day to keep it alive.

### Where is my data stored?
Your meals are stored on your device and (when you post) on secure Firebase servers. We never sell your data or share it with advertisers.
